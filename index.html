Our Sweet Journey

Established in 1985

Maurya Misthan Bhandar has been serving authentic Indian sweets for over three decades, using traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a beloved destination for sweet lovers across the region. We use only the finest ingredients and traditional methods to create sweets that capture the true essence of Indian mithai.
